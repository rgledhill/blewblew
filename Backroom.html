XsonazX in the Backroom

XSONAZX (/ˈsoʊnəs/) AKA Saul Millan's production handle, mixes LoFi synthesis with free jazz improvisation techniques. Inspired by Southwest Legends DJScrew, and Sun City Girls, XSONAZX explores the vibrant sound of the cassette tape. By using cassette players as performers, Millan creates a unique space for objects and musicians to improvise. The group experiments with free form improvisation, meditation and visual scores. In live performance, Millan references Jazz Workshop Ensembles, featuring a wide array of rotating Jazz musicians. This performance will feature multi instrumentalist Kenji Wagner.
